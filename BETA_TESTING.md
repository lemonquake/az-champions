# 🧪 AZ Champions — Beta Test Guide

Welcome, tester! You're playing an early build of **AZ Champions** by Aljay Leodones.
This checklist covers what to try and what to report.

## Getting started
1. Open the game (see README for the URL / how to run).
2. You start with **Azrin, Raphael Rich, and Yoon Sul**. Clear stage **1-3** to unlock
   Ezekiel Fitz free, and **1-8** for Azrael.
3. Your save is in your browser. **Settings → Export save** before clearing browser data!

## Day-one loop to test (15–20 min)
- [ ] Watch the intro, fight stages 1-1 → 1-5 (the 1-5 ELITE fight should feel harder)
- [ ] Collect the idle chest twice (it fills over time — max 12h)
- [ ] Do a ×10 summon (you start with 10 scrolls)
- [ ] Set your best 5 in **EDIT TEAM** — front row = slots 1–2 (tanks/warriors)
- [ ] Level someone up, equip a gear drop, enhance it once
- [ ] Try one Arena fight and one Tower floor
- [ ] Open **Quests** and claim your points + at least one chest
- [ ] In battle: toggle **AUTO** off and fire an ultimate manually (tap the glowing portrait)
- [ ] Toggle 2× speed

## Things that should be true (bug if not!)
- Progress survives refreshing the page and closing the browser
- Coming back after ≥10 minutes shows the "Welcome back" idle haul
- Daily quests + arena fights reset on a new calendar day
- Faction advantage matters: 🔥 beats 🌿 beats 🌊 beats 🔥, ☀ ↔ ☽ beat each other
- A stage that stomps you should become beatable after leveling / ascending / gearing
- Duplicated champions become "copies" and the ASCEND button lights up on the roster
- Pity: you are guaranteed an Elite within 30 summons

## Store testing
- Diamond Market items (scrolls, caches) use in-game diamonds — safe to test freely.
- **Real-money packs open real PayPal checkout.** In beta, after paying you enter your
  PayPal transaction ID to receive the item. Unless you actually want to support the game,
  just press Cancel.
- Note: the merchant cannot buy from themselves — PayPal blocks self-payments. Test real
  purchases from a different PayPal account.

## What to report (and how)
For every issue: **what you did → what you expected → what happened**, plus your
device/browser and a screenshot if visual.

Especially interested in:
1. **Difficulty spikes** — note the exact stage where you got hard-stuck and your team power
2. **Confusion** — anything you had to figure out by trial and error
3. **3D glitches** — floating gear, broken animations, characters clipping, HP bars drifting
4. **Performance** — battles below smooth 30fps? Phone getting hot? (try Settings → Reduced effects)
5. **Economy feel** — did leveling/summoning feel rewarding or grindy? Where did you stop caring?
6. **The fun question** — when did you want "one more battle", and when did you want to quit?

## Known beta limitations
- Purchases are granted on the honor system (transaction-ID entry) — server verification lands before launch
- No guilds/co-op yet (designed, not built)
- Seasonal resets and server leaderboards are display-only concepts for now
- Audio is fully synthesized — proper soundtrack comes later

Thank you for testing! — Aljay
